Questions Answered

FREQUENTLY
ASKED
QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Orizon, our brands, and how we build accessory ranges at scale across Europe and beyond.

Get In Touch
What is Orizon and what do you do? +
Orizon develops and scales gaming accessory ranges through distribution and retail partnerships — through owned brands and partner programs for IP owners and brands.
Do you only sell licensed products? +
No. Our brands are built on strong core (non-licensed) accessory ranges. Licensed editions are selective additions that complement the core.
Do you operate in Europe only? +
Europe is our core region, and we support international expansion with selected partners. Our operating model is built to scale globally.
What makes Orizon products sustainable? +
Eco-friendly materials, recyclable packaging, and manufacturing processes designed to minimize carbon footprint while ensuring durability and long-term use.
How does Orizon support partners post-launch? +
Comprehensive support including performance tracking, growth strategies, replenishment planning, and a dedicated team ready to assist with any challenge.
